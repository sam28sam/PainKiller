On-Site Audit Booking

Submit a short enquiry. We'll confirm feasibility and dates within 24 hours.

The audit is a full day on-site, followed by a written findings report within 7 working days. Booking begins with a short enquiry — once confirmed, your £475 deposit secures the date.

How booking works

Three steps from enquiry to confirmed booking.

01

Submit your enquiry

Tell me about your site, your operation, what's prompted the booking, and three or four dates that could work. Takes about 5 minutes.

5 minutes · No payment yet
02

Feasibility confirmed

Within 24 working hours, I'll review the enquiry, confirm a date that works for both of us, and send you a Stripe invoice for the £475 deposit. If the audit isn't the right fit, I'll say so and recommend something else.

Within 24h · From me directly
03

Booking confirmed

Once the deposit is paid, your audit date is locked in. You'll receive a confirmation page with everything needed before the visit — site contact, team availability, any documents I'll want sight of beforehand.

Date locked · Audit day prepared

Complete the form below. The questions help me understand whether the audit is the right next step for your operation, and what to look at first if it is. Worth knowing

Three commitments worth making explicit before you book.

UK travel is inclusive

The £950 audit fee covers travel anywhere in mainland UK. Overseas audits are available but flight and accommodation costs are invoiced separately — I'll quote upfront if your site is outside the UK.

Honest fit assessment

If after reviewing your enquiry I don't think a full audit is the right next step — too small, too narrow, better served by Rapid-Resolve — I'll tell you. I'd rather lose the booking than take a fee for the wrong service.

Report delivery within 7 working days

The written findings report is delivered within 7 working days of the audit. The £475 balance payment is invoiced on report delivery — you see the work before you pay the remaining fee.
