What's Included

Everything needed to get a complete picture of your operation.

01
Pre-Audit Intake

A short structured questionnaire sent before the audit day. Covers current KPIs, known pain points, and operational context — so no time is wasted getting up to speed on site.

02
Full Audit Day On-Site

A complete floor-level assessment. Observation across all key areas: goods-in, storage, pick/pack, despatch, and any site-specific workflows.

03
Staff Interviews

Up to 5 interviews with operatives, supervisors, and team leaders. The people on the floor always know where the problems are — we ask them directly.

04
Live Process Mapping

Every key workflow mapped in real time during the audit day. Bottlenecks, handoff failures, and waste points visualised and documented.

05
KPI Benchmarking

Your current operational KPIs benchmarked against sector norms — putting your numbers in context and identifying where you're underperforming most.

06
Written Findings Report

Delivered within 7 working days. Covers: top bottlenecks, quick wins (implementable within 30 days), risk flags, and recommended next steps. Clear and practical — no filler.

07
Risk Flag Summary

Anything identified during the audit that poses an operational, safety-adjacent, or compliance-adjacent risk is flagged clearly and separately.

08
Debrief Call

A 30-minute call the day after report delivery to walk through the findings, answer questions, and discuss what a sensible next move looks like.

Results

What audits like this have delivered.

17%
Reduction in Overtime
Multi-shift operation · ~80 staff

Overtime was being driven by planning gaps and handover failures between shifts — not by genuine volume. Shift structure and workflow were restructured. The overtime requirement fell 17% within eight weeks.

22%
Increase in Pick Rate
Retail warehouse · e-commerce fulfilment

Poor product slotting and wasted travel time were the root cause — not picker performance. Picking logic was restructured and movement waste reduced. Pick rate increased 22% with the same headcount.

28%
Faster Goods-In to Shelf
FMCG operation · high-throughput site

Delays across intake, checking, and putaway were creating downstream drag. The intake flow was redesigned to remove waiting time at the key handoff points. Goods reached shelf 28% faster within six weeks.

All results anonymised. Sector, site size, and context available on request.
